Scones

Everyone’s got their own go-to scone recipe. Whether it’s one passed down through generations in a hand-written book, or one you’ve bookmarked and used for years, we all seem to have an opinion on what makes a great scone.

Ingredients

  • 500g self raising flour
  • 250ml chilled lemonade
  • 300ml thickened cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220C and line baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place flour in a large bowl and make well in centre. Pour in lemonade and 1 cup of cream. Use a flat knife to stir until soft dough forms.
  3. Turn dough out onto a floured surface. Knead gently for 30 seconds until smooth. Press into 3cm disc. Use 5cm pastry cutter to cut 20 scones. Place scones on prepared tray.
  4. Bake for 16-18 minutes until scones are risen and golden. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
  5. Serve scone warm or at room temperature with whipped cream and jam, if desired.
