Lemonade

Got lemons? You know what they say...this sweet lemonade is just whats needed to quench summer thirsts.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups caster sugar
  • 1.25 litres lemon juice (3kg lemons)
  • 2 litres cold water
  • Crushed ice to serve
  • 1 lemon, sliced, to serve

Steps

  1. Place sugar, lemon juice and water in a large saucepan over medium heat, Cook, stirring, for 15 minutes. Remove from heat, Cool for 20 minutes.
  2. Transfer to heatproof jugs. Cover, then refrigerate overnight.
  3. Add crushed ice and lemon slices to lemonade. Serve.
